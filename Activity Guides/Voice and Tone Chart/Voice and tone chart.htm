Product principles

Product principles shape the experience presented to the user, and are conveyed by the voice. For example, a public transit app that is used by millions of city residents may aim to be trustworthy, accurate, and approachable. These three principles inform design choices, including writing decisions.

  • Punctuation and capitalization are the mechanics of writing, but can also change based on the principles in play.
